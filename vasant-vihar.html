About New R.S.J. Public School

New R.S.J. (Recreational Study Junction) Public School at Vasant Viahr Jhusi, Allahabad had a very humble begining in the year 2001. The School Mainly caters to the requirments of student who requier pre-primery education. The School run into two shifts. The first shift caters to pre pimeary requirment and second shift caters to class I to V requirment. Since it’s inception, the school has emboldened the idea of it’s being a secular place of learning and has always remined open for all the sections of sociaty. The school’s moto ‘Forward Ever’ is the basic ideal under which the ethics along with the parents and future requirments of all those associated with the school, so that the march of Moving forward continues to make success stories. The School is founded on the basis of co-education system. School provides its students an environment which facilitates then to reach beyond their social and economic status.

The credit for the success, which the school has achieved in small periods of time, gose to the staff of the school as well as the students and their parents who have contributed immensely in deriving new techniques of imparting education. It is the auspicious nexus which has also contributed hugely to the growth of students in extra curricular activities. The school has always been ready for introduction of newer and better schemes for comprehensive and balance development of its students. The school takes no grant in aid from the government and depends entirely upon the money obtained as fee from its pupil.

Our Faculty

As per the guidelines the school maintains highly-qualified and trained staff, which not only implements the curriculum but also inculcates enthusiasm for learning among the students. Due to maintenance of approprite student teacher ratio, the interaction of teachers with the students facilitates involvment teachers with the students in academic and co-curricular activities. The faculty is at par with best available with the aim to achieve excellence.

School Assembly

School begins with Morning Assembly with different Special Presentations fixed day-wise and is an essential part of the School schedule. Each class/section has its own special day to conduct/participate in the assembly. It provides a special window of exposer to every student.

For the purposes of academic administration and with the intent of improving efficiency, the school is devided in three admintrative wings.

Each wing has seperate supervisors in form of Incharge/s. School realises importance of it’s role in giving a good start to the toddlers. It is here where the foundation of learning is laid and strengthened.

Junior School Wing

At the Junior School level unconventional system of education is adopted by the School where education is imparted through play-way methods and concepts are taught via clay modelling, sand play and painting. Teachers also enrich the curriculum through music, art, library and Audio-visual programs.

Middle School Wing

Middle school wing ensures to provide the students all necessary inputs which makes themselves adept at any field from Languages, Mathematics, Science, Arts and Technology or Performing Arts. It takes care of the increasing range of opportunities available now a days. Spacious and airy classrooms makes learning comfortable. Though the basic principles and theories are taught in the classroom but habit to question with the intent to clarify doubts are inculcated.

Senior School Wing

Senior Wing of the school strives at maintaing excellence in the field of academics to enable the students to stand at par with competitive world while remaining in conformity with the CBSE-CCE pattern of education.

Extra-Curricular Activities

PROCESS

Registration for Admission: Collection of the Registration Form from the school office on payment of 200/- Submission of the duly filled Registration Form, with necessary documents as detailed in the form, within five days from the date of purchase. ADMISSION POLICY

Admissions to class Nursery is being granted on first come first serve basis, till the availability of seats. Admissions to other classes are subject to availabilty of seats created by the transfer of regular students or Drop-outs. The avaiblity of seats shall be duly notified here & the school notice board after declaration of results for the academic session 2023 - 2024. However for admission to other classes students must bring records of the previous two completed classes along with the necessary registration. After the valid registration interested students shall be called upon to take enterance test.

Our Facilities

Discover the facilities at the New R.S.J. Public School.

COMPUTER LAB

The School has a well-equipped computer lab. The School has competent computer teachers to train the students in the latest advancements in technology and computing. Beginning from the Junior School, we focus on keyboarding, word processing and internet skills. The computer lab is equipped with the facilities like, Multimedia systems, LCD monitors, Supervised Internet connection, online UPS for system backup, educational softwares etc.

SMART CLASS

The School management have been successful in bringing technology in classrooms with the introduction of interactive Whiteboards and Smart Boards. These allow students to interact with the lesson content through the softwares provided by EDUCOMP. This helps in widening their analytical faculties. Classrooms are spacious and have adequate cooling and lighting through fans and tubelights. The number of students in each class is restricted, so that the teachers can give individual attention to each student.

LIBRARY

The School library houses more than 10000 books apart from the reference section for the students as well as teachers. No doubt it is a learning center for all. To keep the students updated with the current affairs, the school provides at least four newspapers daily apart from several other periodicals on variety of subjects. The library also provides e-books and e-learning facility. It also houses several other books to help the senior students for their competetive exams.

SCIENCE LAB

The School offers well equipped modern labs for Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics & Social-Studies as per the settled standards. It helps students in inculcating scientific temper through controlled experiments as per the guidelines. The Labs are also linked through the various softwares wherein student can get first hand experience of experiments.

LANGUAGE LAB

The School operates language lab through the make shift area of the library with the sole purpose of improving phonetic skills of the students. It houses audio-video section through audio and video technique with the help of documentaries and films pertaining to various languages.

MEDICAL ROOM

The School has a reasonable equipped Medical Room to meet the emergency situation. A Registered Medical Practitioner visits the School daily during school hours. All emergencies are attended with utmost care. If required, the student is also sent to nearby nursing home/hospital. Regular medical checkups are conducted.

TRANSPORT

The School has its own fleet of buses. Each bus has a driver and a conductor for complete safety and security of the children. Each bus is provided with mobile phone facility. For the safety of students every bus is equipped with fire-extinguishers. The routes and timing of the entire fleet of buses are monitored with the help of staff appointed for the same. Bus facility is available on limited routes.

ARTS

School provides for it’s students an appropriate venue for training, learning & participation in Dance (Indian & Western), Music (Indian and Western), Theatre, Performing Arts etc. The results of participation in such activities are improved self-confidence, better public speaking skills etc. Students also show other gains as well, such as the “ability to work with an ensemble in cooperative ventures” and the “ability to work through consensus and differences or obstacles to achieve a goal”.

BOOK/STATIONARY

The book run inside the school primises for limited period to faciliatate the students for their purchase of curriculum books & stationary designed to support the child’s development right from Nursery to Class XII student.
